Assemble the Front Panel

For this section you will need:

Parts

  • 8 10 cm Male-Female Jumpers
  • 4 20 cm Female-Female Jumpers
  • 2 LCD Screen
  • 2 M3 - 15mm screws
  • 2 M3 nut
  • 1 Membrane 4x4 Keypad

Printed parts

  • 1 Front Panel
  • 1 Lcd Adapter

Step 1: Secure the LCD screen and adapter

  • Take the LCD screen adapter and LCD Screen.
  • Attach the LCD Screen to the LCD screen adapter.

Step 2: Attach to the Front Panel

  • Secure the screen with 2 M3 - 15mm screws and M3 nuts in the top two holes of the front panel.

Step 3: Prepare the Keypad

  • Take the membrane 4x4 keypad and peel off the backing, ensuring that all the paper is removed.

Step 4: Fix the Keypad on the Front Panel

  • Pass the cables through the hole on the front panel and securely attach the membrane 4x4 keypad in place.

Step 5: Install the LCD Screen Jumpers

  • Take 4 20 cm Female-Female Jumpers.
  • Insert the jumpers into the 4 connectors on the back of the LCD Screen.

Step 6: Install the Keypad Jumpers

  • Take 8 10 cm Male-Female Jumpers.
  • Insert the jumpers into the 8 connectors on the Membrane 4x4 Keypad.

The Assembled Front Panel is now ready.
