Assemble everything together

For this section you will need:

Parts

  • 1 Assembled Front Panel
  • 1 Assembled Interface Base

Electronic Components

Mechanical Components

  • 4 M3 nut
  • 2 M3x10 screw
  • 2 M3x15 screw

Step 1: Connect the LCD Screen to the Raspberry Pi Pico

  • Take the Assembled Front Panel and locate the 4 20 cm Female-Female Jumpers coming out of the Lcd Screen.
  • Connect these jumpers to the corresponding pins of the Raspberry Pi Pico in the Assembled Interface Base, following the wiring diagram.

Step 2: Connect the Membrane Keypad to the Raspberry Pi Pico

  • Locate the 8 10 cm Male-Female Jumpers coming out of the Membrane 4x4 Keypad.
  • Connect these jumpers to the respective pins of the Raspberry Pi Pico in the Assembled Interface Base, following the wiring diagram.

Step 3: Fix the Front Panel in Place

  • Take 4 M3 nut and slide them onto the 4 sockets of the Assembled Interface Base.
  • Use 2 M3x10 screw to secure the Assembled Front Panel to the Assembled Interface Base in the 2 bottom holes.
  • Use 2 M3x15 screw to secure the Assembled Front Panel to the Assembled Interface Base in the 2 middle top holes.
