Interface

The assembly is broken up into several steps:

  1. Print the plastic parts
  2. Assemble the Front Panel
  3. Assemble the Main Electronics
  4. Assemble everything together

For this section you will need:

Parts

  • 6 M3 - 10mm screws
  • 2 M3 - 15mm screws
  • 1 M3 - 25mm screws

Electronic Components

  • 7 10 cm Female-Female Jumpers
  • 8 10 cm Male-Female Jumpers
  • 12 20 cm Female-Female Jumpers
  • 1 40 Pin Header
  • 1 5.5mm Female Jack Connector
  • 2 LCD Screen
  • 1 Membrane 4x4 Keypad
  • 1 Raspberry Pi Pico
  • 2 TMC2209 Driver
  • 1 Voltage Regulator

Mechanical Components

  • 14 M3 nut
  • 2 M3x10 screw
  • 2 M3x15 screw
